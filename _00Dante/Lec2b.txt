During the time of Dante Florence was a densly populated city surrounded by a wall.
Life was primarily lived outdoors and the citizens knew each others faces, 
basically you lived your life closer to your neighbours then nowdays.
The old aristocrate disliked the new rich, and disputes between people oftently
started by som minor public dispute. Contemporary chronicles tells that facionalism
in Florence started 1215 by a bridgrom snupping of a bride for a better offer. This
public insult was answered by the killing of the bridegroom. The bad blood lasted
into Dantes time and was usually held much alive by the struggle for political power.
. Moreover, Florence was an independent city state (Italay was not of course 
unified until the 19th century). Florence, like other city states, usually was
in a perpetual struggle of varying intensity with its neighbouring citystates.
